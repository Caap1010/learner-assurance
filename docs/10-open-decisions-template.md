# Open Decisions and Sign Off Tracker

## How to Use

- Record unresolved decisions before build start.
- Assign owner and due date.
- Mark status as Open, In Review, or Closed.

## Sign Off Checklist

- Product scope sign-off complete
- Permission matrix sign-off complete
- Data dictionary sign-off complete
- Workflow and SLA sign-off complete
- KPI definition sign-off complete
- Security and retention sign-off complete
- UAT entry criteria signed
- Production release authority confirmed
